This lesson is designed for students beginning research in the TCID, IA, or Ph.D. in Technical Communication programs at IIT, aiming to enhance or refresh their research skills through efficient strategies.

Getting Started

Learn how to access IIT library services and basic functionality on the IIT library website.

Accessing IIT Library Services

You need your Hawk Card number to access certain IIT library services. Even without it, you can access online resources through the library website.

Accessing Library Resources

Log into myIIT and select "Library Resources" to connect via a proxy server to access library resources directly.
